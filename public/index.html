+ Instructions for use:

  1. Choose a recording from the drop down menu next to "Choose a piece."
  2. Listen as much as you like to the recording to prepare for annotation.
  3. When ready to annotate, press the "start annotation" button.
    • Annotations will only be recorded while the recording is playing.
    • Annotation can be halted at any time with the "stop annotation" button.
    • If you wish to restart your annotation, use the "clear and restart" button (annotation must be off).
  4. Choose from the colored emotion buttons to annotate the recording with the appropriate emotion.
    • Buttons can be pressed with the mouse, or changed with the numbers in parentheses. For example, you can switch to "Fear" by pressing 2 on your keyboard or "Joy" by pressing 9.
  5. Once annotation is complete, submit your annotation with the "Complete and send to server" button below.
