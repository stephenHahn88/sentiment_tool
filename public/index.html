⏶ Instructions for use:

  1. Choose a recording from the drop down menu next to "Choose a piece."
  2. Listen as much as you like to the recording to prepare for annotation.
  3. When ready to annotate, press the "start annotation" button.
    • The chosen piece will be locked until the page is reloaded or "clear and restart" is pressed
    • Annotations will only be recorded while the recording is playing.
    • Annotation can be halted at any time with the "stop annotation" button.
    • If you wish to restart your annotation, use the "clear and restart" button (annotation must be off).
  4. Choose from the large colored emotion buttons to annotate the recording with the appropriate emotion. Smaller colored bubbles show words that belong to the larger, primary emotions - you can look to these words if you have trouble associating a primary emotion with the music.
    • Choose the emotion that best fits each passage in your opinion. There are no wrong answers.
    • Buttons can be pressed with the mouse, or changed with the numbers in parentheses. For example, you can switch to "Fear" by pressing 2 on your keyboard or "Joy" by pressing 9.
  5. If there are any concerns with your analysis, the recording, or bugs, please write your comment(s) in the text area below your analysis.
  6. Once annotation is complete, submit your annotation with the "Complete and send to server" button below.
  7. Check out previous analyses here (under construction)
